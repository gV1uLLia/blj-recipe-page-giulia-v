Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

28 Cookies
backing sheets, 2-3 baking trays
  • 110g
    Soft Butter
  • 155g
    Sugar
  • 1/4 Pack
    Vanilla Sugar
  • 1
    Egg
  • 1/4 TS
    Salt
  • 150g
    Dark Chocolate chopped or cubes
  • 175g
    Flour
  • 1 knife tip
    Backing Powder

Step 1

First stir the butter until peaks form. It should look something like this:
Add Sugar, Vanilla Sugar Salt and Egg(s) and stir until the dough lightens. It should also look something like this:
In the next step mix the Flour and the baking powder in a separate bowl. Seven the Flour and the baking powder into the dough and mix it. After mixing everything add the chocolate and stir again.

Step 2

Tipp: If you put the finished dough into the freezer for about half an hour the dough will be easier to form. Take two spoons to form balls, in the size of a tree nut, out of the dough. Spread them evenly on a baking sheet and put it on a baking tray.

Step 3

Preheat the oven to 155 degrees Celsius hot air or 175 degrees Celsius upper and lower heat. Bake in the middle of the oven for 11 - 13 minutes. Take them out and let them cool off until they harden.

Enjoy!
